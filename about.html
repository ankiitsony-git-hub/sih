About Crowdsourced Civic Issue Reporting And Resolution System

Who We Are

CCIRRSS is a digital initiative to empower citizens, workers, and government agencies to collaborate towards cleaner cities. Our mission is to simplify complaint reporting, ensure transparency, and enable faster resolution of civic issues.

Our Mission

To provide a seamless platform for reporting civic issues with real-time tracking, ensuring cleaner environments and empowered citizens.

Our Vision

A future where every city is clean, sustainable, and governed with transparency and accountability.
